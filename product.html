Who is SkillCorner? SkillCorner is a broadcast tracking data provider for football matches and training sessions. They use powerful Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning tools to help teams gain a competitive edge by providing the most accurate broadcast tracking data and advanced integrated analytics on players and the team. SkillCorner then uploads this information onto an ever-growing database and finally also displaying the data on readable graphs and infographics. All this to help the players, coaches, and managers to improve their tactics and overall performance on the pitch. They are currently one of the market leaders for broadcasting data providers for football.

...

What problem does SkillCorner solve? Event data and tracking data are the keys to unlocking football performance. But until now, tracking data has been expensive to collect, time intensive, and not available across different leagues and competitions.SkillCorner is changing that by providing teams access to exclusive player and ball tracking data without the usual restrictions. In just a click, our technology lets you access dynamic and contextualised insights that can power your recruitment process as well as match or opposition analysis.

...

How does SkillCorner work? The software uses AI-powered video tracking technology that uses computer vision and machine learning to generate tracking data from any football broadcast. The video tracking technology is capable of recognizing, positioning, and following in real time, the football players, the referee, and even the ball from any broadcasted game. They focus on four main aspects which helps put everything together to gather the most accurate data possible, the aspects are, Camera Pose Estimation, Object Detection, Multi Object Tracking, Player Recognition.

...

Are there any top clubs work with SkillCorner? Yes, initially SkillCorner was working with lower league football clubs which is understandable as there were new to the market and not well known. Also because they had improvements to be made and learn as they go. However around 2020-21 SkillCorner and Liverpool FC started collaborating for a period of time of 1 year long to hone the accuracy of the algorithm before agreeing to a partnership. Since then, SkillCorner has begun working with other clubs in the Premier League, Italy´s Series A, La Liga, and France´s Ligue 1.

What can i expect from SkillCorner? Access to simple to use and objective data on the physical profile of more than 20 000 players from an ever-growing list of 46 competitions globally. Player data such as, distances and number of runs in different speed zones, accelerations, speed, in-out possessions etc. Also allows the ability to compare physically any competition, team or player. Finally, SkillCorner data; fully automated and real time data collection, 98% more accurate than in-stadium tracking systems, integrated analytics (physical, tactical, and technical), Flexible data delivery via API Integration or Web Query Tool.

The future of SkillCorner and their AI data analysis will revolve around trying to make the best football analysis software on the market. They will work on their algorythms, hardware such as camera equipment and sensors, as well as on the broadcasting side of their product. Besides overall improvements, they will continue to collaborate with other data analytic companies such as Twenty3 whom they have recently teamed up with. Twenty3 is another creator of AI sports data which will see physical data collected by SkillCorner added to their toolbox. SkillCorner are illustrative of the changing of the guard in the approach to data analytics.
